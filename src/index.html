Welcome.

The place for for new students. Save time, no more maze at P35

What problem do we solve?

One may find it confusing to find the needed room at the beginning of the school year, especially when you have a lot in your mind so our web-site helps to find some useful places quickly.

All You have to know when you cross the entrance door for the first time is that our building P35 has everything that a student may need:

  • Cantine and Sitting areas,
  • Library and Classrooms,
  • Gym and a Climbing wall,
  • Group-rooms and silent areas.
It may be confusing to find your way to the needed area, tough.

Use maps like a pro

We made maps so that the new students can find their way out easily, all information is collected in one place. The site also shows hiddend places that are difficult to find for new students, and frequently used places, like a copy machines, water coolers etc.

It's designed to help people orient themselves around using simplified maps. There are 8 floors plus basement, on the maps site. Every area is designed to perform different functions.

In the basement and on the ground floor you'll find places to eat, relax (gym, climbing wall), and freshen yourself (bathrooms).

The second floor is mainly dedicated for the students taking Forkurs or Treterminsordning.
