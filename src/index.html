Welcome.

Here students can find different useful areas on every floor at P35.

What problem do we solve?

Web site shows the structure of building at P35 and some hidden places that are difficult to find for new students.

Its difficult to orient in a new place, especially when you have a lot in your mind so our web-site helps to find some useful places quickly.

We add pictures of every floor to make it easy.

Solution

Solution is a web site that shows via maps and text where new students can find different frequently used places, like a copy machines, water coolers etc.

We made maps to orient students easily, all information is collected in one place.
