What do symbols on the map mean?

This one means Emergency Exit only. We have marked some of the least intuitive Emergency Exits in the building. They may seem to be hidden, however once You have seen one of those, it will become easier for You to remember.

Why are rooms marked with letters? What do those letters mean?

As You have noticed we have rooms with different letters in front of the room number. Those letters are short for the street name, because each classroom is placed in different building area, which has a neighboring street.
