Third floor

Library and Læringssenteret are placed on the third floor. At Læringssenteret you have place to learn and there are some computers available for student use. Access to unisex bathroom at both Library and Læringssenteret. On this floor You’ll find classrooms by exiting the elevator and going to the left, through the Læringssenteret, and going through the door near student lockers. Those classrooms are numbered from PH355 – to PH374.
