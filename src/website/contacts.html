Who are we?

We are first year students from Oslo and Akershus University college faculty of software engeneering, and are a group on four. We are interested in solving problems that new students might face after starting.

Our website is a proposition of a solution

Our group has worked on desiging simple maps to make it easier for new students and other newbies
to use the building. This is more a presentation of an idea, which can be further developed and we hope
used at our school.
We wanted to do something useful for our local community. This website is a fruit of countless
hours spent on designing the website, walking through the building and looking for places designated
for the students, and we drew it in for of these maps.
If You spot any missing information do not hesitate to contact us. Well keep on updating the website
until You're fully satisfied!
We hope You'll have a great use of this website! Thank You!
